Wonder World: BETA
Your goal for now: dig deep, find Gold, craft a full set of Gold Armor (helmet, chestplate, leggings, boots), and make it back to the surface alive.
Movement
  • W A S D — move
  • Mouse — look around
  • Space — jump / swim up
  • Shift — sprint
  • Ctrl or Q — sneak / descend
Interacting
  • Left-click — mine blocks & attack
  • Right-click — use the held item: place a block, eat food, draw a bow, or open a station (crafting table, oven, smelter, chest)
  • Stuck with your head in a block? Just mine — it digs out the block at your head first
Inventory & Crafting
  • E — open inventory  ·  C — crafting / creative menu
  • 1–0 — select a hotbar slot
  • Crafting Table — tools, armor & blocks  ·  Oven — cook food  ·  Smelter — ingots & glass
  • Mine ore, smelt it into ingots, then craft armor and tools
Survival
  • Keep an eye on Health, Hunger and Energy
  • Hold Right-click with food to eat (watch the eat bar)
  • Don't suffocate inside blocks, and beware fall damage in deep caves
  • Gold is found near the very bottom of the world — pack food before you dig
Menus
  • Esc — close a menu, or pause the game
  • The game only responds to movement while the mouse is locked (click the screen to lock)
